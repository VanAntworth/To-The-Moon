True or False ?

Do you think one person can change the way the market trends?
Does Elon Musk have the ability to sway certain stock and crypto in his favor?

It is undoubtable that Elon Musk is a globally know celebrity. Our story starts with one of our team members making financial gains through following the tweeted advice of Elon Musk. It is a crazy thought that social media and specifically twitter could have the power to sway a nation.

Do you believe his tweets could have an effect on stock value or are the trends that have been seen a coincidence?
This Project is an attempt to figure out if his tweeted words can in fact have an impact on the market or not? . Through NLP we have worked to predict the power that his words (on the social media platform like Twitter) have on near future values of the stocks and cryptocurrency we relate him to.

Twitter (Stock) On April 25, Twitter’s board of directors accepted Musk’s buyout offer of $44 billion. On July 8, Musk announced that he intended to terminate the proposed acquisition.
