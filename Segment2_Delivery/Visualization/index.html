About

It has been observed that Elon Musk tweets have been impacting the Stocks and Cryptocurrency market. We wanted to analyze the impact of the tweets and how long the impact was holding true. This project will predict the Stock or Cryptocurrencys (Tesla, Twitter and Dogecoin data) future value, and also show the impact of tweets for respective stock/crypto. We are embarking on a quest to find the influence Elon Musk tweet's have had over Doge Coin. In the process we are interested in understanding how the use of language by a single individual with a large following, can influence the price of stocks and coins.
