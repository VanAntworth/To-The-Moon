True or False ?

Do you think one person can change the way the market trends?
Does Elon Musk have the ability to sway certain stock and crypto in his favor?

It is undoubtable that Elon Musk is a globally know celebrity. Our story starts with one of our team members making financial gains through following the tweeted advice of Elon Musk. It is a crazy thought that social media and specifically twitter could have the power to sway a nation.

Do you believe his tweets could have an effect on stock value or are the trends that have been seen a coincidence?
This Project is an attempt to figure out if his tweeted words can in fact have an impact on the market or not? . Through NLP we have worked to predict the power that his words (on the social media platform like Twitter) have on near future values of the stocks and cryptocurrency we relate him to.

We have decided to use data from three stocks/coin that we have found relation to with Elon Musk The three are:

Dogecoin (Cryptocurrency) - An insignificant crypto currency that grew to fame in April 2019, Musk tweeted “Dogecoin might be my fav cryptocurrency. It’s pretty cool.” The cryptocurrency gained immense traction as Musk continued to endorse and hype it on social media. According to Decrypt, dogecoin searches spiked from 2019 through 2020 around the times Musk tweeted about it.

Tesla (Stock)-Tesla designs and manufactures electric vehicles (electric cars and trucks), battery energy storage from home to grid-scale, solar panels and solar roof tiles. The company was created by Elon Musk and he currently owns 17 percent of the company.

Twitter (Stock) On April 25, Twitter’s board of directors unanimously accepted Musk’s buyout offer of $44 billion, with the company set to be privatized. On July 8, Musk announced that he intended to terminate the proposed acquisition.
