True or False ?

Do you think one person can change the way the market trends?
Does Elon Musk have the ability to sway certain stock and crypto in his favor?


It is undoubtable that Elon Musk is a globally known celebrity. Our story starts with one of our team members making financial gains through following the tweeted advice of Elon Musk. It is a crazy thought that social media and specifically Twitter could have the power to sway a nation.

Do you believe his tweets could have an effect on stock value or are the trends that have been seen a coincidence?

This Project is an attempt to figure out if his tweeted words can in fact have an impact on the market. Through NLP we have worked to predict the power that his words (on the social media platform like Twitter) have on near future values of the stocks and cryptocurrency we relate him to.

We have chosen three stocks/coin that we find relation to with Elon Musk. The three are:

Dogecoin (Cryptocurrency) - An insignificant crypto currency that grew to fame in April 2019 when Musk tweeted “Dogecoin might be my fav cryptocurrency. It’s pretty cool.” Doge gained immense traction as Musk continued to endorse and hype it on social media.

Tesla (Stock) - Tesla is best known for designing and manufacturing electric vehicles. The company was created by Elon Musk and he currently owns 17% of the company. This impacted the stock price of Twitter.

Twitter (Stock) - On April 25, Twitter’s board of directors accepted Musk’s buyout offer of $44 billion. On July 8, Musk announced that he intended to terminate the proposed acquisition.
